Notion

The notetaking app that can do a lot more than advertised

Our rating: 8/10

Imagine Microsoft OneNote, but on steroids. Not everyone has access to the Office package and there are limited options out there for notetaking and project management which doesn't leave something to be desired. Notion aims to fix that.

How does it function?

Notion is actually both a notetaking app and a project management app with a ton of features. It can be used just for easy notetaking that updates between all your devices, but you can also use it for groupwork and delegate tasks within a team and coordinate deadlines.
