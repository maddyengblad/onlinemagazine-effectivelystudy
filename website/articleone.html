Studiz

The student discount app you've always wanted

Our rating: 8/10

We've all been through this, we get a student discount card, put it in the wallet, hidden and forgotten until the day we graduate. We suddenly remember the card and wonder “Huh, I wonder how much money I could've saved if I used it?”

How does it function?

Studiz is a website and an app that tells you exactly where you can get student discounts and how much the discount is. It has a map function, so when you're out and about, it's very convenient and easy to just look on the map and see which nearby stores offers a student discount. There's a huge variety of categories and I personally wouldn't buy anything online without using Studiz, as it's not only student discounts but it conveniently tells you other discount codes for whichever site you're shopping on.
