Grammarly

Having problems writing? Grammarly has your back

Our rating: 9/10

Have you ever wanted someone to proof-read your work, but you don't really wanna ask someone? Grammarly is that friend who never says no and will always be there no matter when and where.

How does it function?

Grammarly is an AI assistant that can help you with spelling, grammar, punctuation, mistakes, plagiarism detecter and can even suggest better wording to help make you sound more professional. When you create an account you're asked a couple clarifying questions like what style of tone you're going for when writing.
